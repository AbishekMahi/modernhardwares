Why We Are Special?

At Modern Hardwares, our goal is to provide high-quality hardware and plywood products to the people of Kovilpatti at an affordable price. We started small in 2008, but our commitment to quality has always been our top priority. Thanks to the support of the people of Kovilpatti, we have grown into a leading provider of hardware and plywood products.

Our product range includes everything from hinges and locks to plywood, MDF, laminates, PVC laminates, acrylic laminates, chimneys, safes, and even modular kitchens. We only stock the best brands, ensuring that our customers receive the highest quality products available.

We are proud to have been serving the people of Kovilpatti for over adecade and we lookforward to continuing our commitment to providing top-notch hardware and plywood productsfor years to come. Our customers can always count on us for the best products at the rightprice, and we are dedicated to providing the highest level of customer service.
